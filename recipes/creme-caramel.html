creme caramel Recipe

FULL CREME CARAMEL RECIPE

description

creme caramel is a french dessert that is loved worldwide

its worth to mention that its made of two main ingredients flan and the caramel itself

ingredients

ingredients for the caramel

  • 1/2 cup (105 g) sugar
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) water

ingredients for the flan

  • 1/4 cup (55 g) sugar
  • 2 tsp (10 ml) vanilla extract
  • 2 cups (500 ml) milk, warm
  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten

STEPS

With the rack in the middle position, preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).

how to make the CARAMEL

  1. In a saucepan over medium-high heat, heat the sugar and water without stirring until an amber caramel forms. Divide the caramel among six 1/2-cup (125 ml) ramekins. Let cool.

how to make the FLAN

  1. in another saucepan, bring the milk, sugar and vanilla to a boil. Stir until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Meanwhile, in a bowl, beat the eggs. Gradually whisk in the warm milk mixture. Pour into the ramekins.
  3. Prepare a water bath by laying a tea towel in the bottom of a large baking dish.
  4. Remove the ramekins from the baking dish. Let cool. Cover and refrigerate for 6 hours or until completely cooled.
  5. Run the blade of a knife around the ramekins, invert onto a plate to unmould and serve very cold.
